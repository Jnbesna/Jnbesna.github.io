CHM2501: Biological Chemistry

Undergraduate chemistry course - Arts and Science Faculty

Notions of chemical organization in biological systems. Structure, physical properties, reactivity, biomolecules analysis and applications. Introduction to the fields of biophysical, bioanalytical, bioorganic and bioinorganic chemistry.

Undergraduate chemistry course - Arts and Science Faculty

Conjugated compounds; polyenes and aromatics. UV-visible spectroscopy. Electrophilic aromatic substitution and concerted reactions. Biologically significant compounds. Advanced NMR spectroscopy.
